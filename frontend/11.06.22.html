MOST POPULAR HOCKEY PLAYER FROM ESTONIA

Playing Career

After playing with different junior teams,
Komarov began his professional career in the Finnish SM-liiga with Ässät. After winning a silver medal with Ässät in 2006, he moved to Pelicans. After his season with Ässät, he was chosen in the sixth round of the 2006 NHL Entry Draft, 180th overall, by the Toronto Maple Leafs. Komarov captained Finland at the 2007 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships
in Leksand and Mora, Sweden. During the 2009–10 season, Komarov played with Dynamo Moscow of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).
Komarov won a gold medal with Finland at the 2011 IIHF World Championships in Bratislava and Košice, Slovakia.
